I am a Master’s student in the Computer Engineering Department of the Sharif University of Technology (SUT), where I serve as a Research Assistant at the Image Processing Lab under the supervision of Prof. Kasaei.

In the summer of 2022, I was a Research Intern at Image and Signal Processing Group, UCLouvain, under the supervision of Prof. De Vleeschouwer. Before joining SUT, I completed my Bachelor’s in Computer Engineering at Isfahan University of Technology (IUT). I was an undergraduate Research Assistant in the Computer Vision Lab under the supervision of Dr. Karimi.

My research addresses visual recognition tasks like Multi-Object Tracking, Person Re-Identification, and Semantic Segmentation. I am mainly working on the Tracking and Segmentation of players in team sports videos, specifically in soccer videos. Besides, I am also interested in compacting deep networks and transferring knowledge through Knowledge Distillation. Furthermore, my research interests include Self-supervised Learning.
